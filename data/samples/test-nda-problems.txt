MUTUAL CONFIDENTIALITY AND NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT

This Agreement is made between:

GlobalTech Ventures Inc., a corporation incorporated in the State of Delaware, USA, with offices at 350 Fifth Avenue, New York, NY 10118 ("GlobalTech"); and

PortSwigger Ltd ("PortSwigger").

RECITALS

The Parties desire to explore a potential business collaboration and in connection therewith may share certain proprietary and confidential information.

1. CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION

For purposes of this Agreement, "Confidential Information" means any and all information, without limitation, disclosed by either Party to the other, in any form or medium, including but not limited to technical data, trade secrets, know-how, research, product plans, products, services, customers, markets, software, developments, inventions, processes, formulas, technology, designs, drawings, engineering, hardware configuration, marketing, finances, or other business information. For the avoidance of doubt, all information shared between the Parties shall be presumed confidential regardless of how it was communicated.

2. OBLIGATIONS

Each Receiving Party shall hold all Confidential Information in strict confidence and shall not disclose it to any third party whatsoever. The Receiving Party shall not use the Confidential Information for any purpose other than evaluating the potential collaboration. The Parties acknowledge that any disclosure of Confidential Information would cause irreparable harm for which monetary damages would be an inadequate remedy.

3. TERM

The obligations of confidentiality set out in this Agreement shall remain in effect in perpetuity, without limitation of time, and shall survive termination or expiry of this Agreement indefinitely.

4. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY AND WORK PRODUCT

Any work product, inventions, developments, improvements, or deliverables created by either Party in connection with the Purpose, or arising from any use of the other Party's Confidential Information, shall be owned exclusively by GlobalTech. Each Party hereby irrevocably assigns all rights, title, and interest in and to any such work product to GlobalTech. PortSwigger acknowledges that all work performed under this Agreement constitutes work made for hire for the benefit of GlobalTech.

5. NO LIMITATIONS ON LIABILITY

In the event of any breach of this Agreement, the breaching Party shall be liable for all losses, damages, costs, and expenses suffered by the non-breaching Party, including indirect, special, incidental, consequential, and punitive damages, without any cap or limitation whatsoever. The Parties expressly waive any limitation on liability that might otherwise apply under applicable law.

6. INDEMNIFICATION

PortSwigger shall defend, indemnify, and hold harmless GlobalTech, its officers, directors, employees, and agents from and against any and all claims, demands, losses, liabilities, damages, fines, penalties, costs, and expenses (including reasonable attorneys' fees) arising out of or relating to: (a) any breach of this Agreement by PortSwigger; (b) any use of the Confidential Information by PortSwigger; (c) any negligence or wilful misconduct of PortSwigger; or (d) any claim by a third party relating to PortSwigger's business or operations.

7. NON-SOLICITATION

For a period of three (3) years following the termination of this Agreement, PortSwigger shall not, directly or indirectly, solicit, recruit, hire, or employ any person who is at the time, or has been at any time during the preceding twenty-four (24) months, an employee, consultant, contractor, or agent of GlobalTech. This restriction applies to all employees of GlobalTech regardless of their role or involvement in this Agreement.

8. GOVERNING LAW

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of New York, United States of America, without regard to its conflict of law provisions. Any disputes arising under this Agreement shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of New York County, New York.

9. ASSIGNMENT

GlobalTech may freely assign this Agreement, or any of its rights or obligations hereunder, to any third party at its sole discretion and without the consent of PortSwigger.

10. GENERAL

This Agreement may not be amended except by a writing signed by both Parties. If any provision hereof is held unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall remain in effect.

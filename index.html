The Playbook

A structured knowledge base of PortSwigger's established legal positions: governing law, liability caps, IP ownership, payment terms, and more. The AI applies these positions; it doesn't invent them. Every finding names the precedent it used.

02  —  Triage Categories
● Urgent
Needs immediate attention
Contract deviates from established positions in ways that create real legal or commercial risk. Senior review before any signature.
What triggers it
  • Wrong governing law (non-England & Wales)
  • IP assignment to third party
  • Uncapped liability exposure
  • One-sided indemnification clause
  • Perpetual confidentiality with no carve-outs
What the lawyer gets
Flagged clauses with exact quote, established position, and suggested redline ready to paste into the contract.
◐ Review Required
Standard template, some deviations
Broadly standard structure but contains clauses that deviate from agreed positions in ways worth a quick check.
What triggers it
  • Payment terms outside 30-day standard
  • Auto-renewal with short notice window
  • Non-solicitation period beyond 12 months
  • Novel clause with no matching precedent
  • Confidentiality term under 3 years
What the lawyer gets
Summary of deviations, precedent count, recommended action per clause. Typically 2–3 minutes to resolve.
✓ No Action
Boilerplate, matches standard
All clauses match established positions. No deviations. Can be signed off without reading.
What confirms it
  • English law, mutual obligations
  • Standard liability cap (12 months fees)
  • Mutual confidentiality, 3-year term
  • 30-day payment terms
  • Standard carve-outs present
What the lawyer gets
Confirmed position list with precedent counts. Sign-off takes seconds, no reading required.

Novel clauses (those with no matching precedent) are flagged separately and escalated to senior review. Once a decision is made, it becomes the next precedent.

The principle

"Legal arrives pre-briefed and reads only the contracts that need attention. Sales has live deal visibility at every stage. Finance forecasts against what's actually cleared. The same pipeline, used by every team that touches a deal."

05  —  Phased Rollout — Trust Ladder
Phase 1 — Now
AI advises, lawyer reads all
Every contract gets pre-reviewed. The lawyer arrives briefed, knowing exactly where to focus. Sales has real-time visibility on every deal's legal status.
Phase 2 — As trust builds
Lawyer reads flagged only
No-action contracts get signed off from the queue summary. Legal reads only what needs reading. Sales works from a live status feed, so deal visibility stays current even at end of quarter.
Phase 3 — Full confidence
Spot checks on approved
High-confidence contracts clear without a full read. Legal focuses on the deals that actually need them. Finance can forecast against real data — contracts clearing this week are revenue closeable this week.
